Introduction to Graphic Design: Concepts

Design Fundamentals

This certification from linkedin Learning introduces the essential principles of graphic design. The course focused on understanding visual communication and how design elements influence perception, readability, and user engagement. Through this learning experience, I explored the foundational concepts that guide effective visual design across digital platforms, branding, and modern web interfaces.

Figure 1: Certificate of Completion – Introduction to Graphic Design

Core Competencies Developed:

• Visual Hierarchy – Structuring design elements to guide user attention effectively.
• Typography – Understanding type selection, readability, and layout balance.
• Color Theory – Applying color relationships to enhance visual communication.
• Layout Principles – Creating balanced and aesthetically pleasing compositions.
