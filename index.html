About Me

Hi, I'm Jino Guinang. I enjoy transforming ideas into practical and functional designs. My work focuses on turning concepts into precise technical plans that guide real-world construction and development.


My background combines engineering discipline, technical drafting, and a growing passion for technology and digital creation. I enjoy solving spatial challenges, working with tools like AutoCAD and Revit, and continuously improving my technical skills.


Recently, I have also been exploring web development and design, building projects that combine structure, creativity, and functionality. This portfolio showcases my experiences, projects, and the skills I continue to develop.
